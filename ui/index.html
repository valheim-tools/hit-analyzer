How damage taken is calculated

Damage refers to the maximum physicalBlunt, Pierce, Slash or lightning damage a mob can deal. In reality, mob damage varies within a range rather than being a fixed value.

1 Raw damage is scaled by difficulty, star & extra damage bonuses → Effective Damage
2 Effective damage is reduced by block armor → Block-Reduced Damage
3 Block reduced damage is reduced by body armor → Final Damage
4 Final damage is subtracted from Max Health → Remaining Health
